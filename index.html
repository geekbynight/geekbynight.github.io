Overview

The visualsation showes the average spending of different departments in US government from 1992-2014.

The data of this line plots is sourced from the dataset that details projects and associated costs that were commisioned by the US Government, the X Axis represents the time, the Y Axis represents the lifecycle cost of projects, which are grouped by different departments of the government. From the visualisation we can see that from 2000~2001, the Department of Defense started taking up a large part of the total spendign of US government, and the Department of Agriculture and the Department of State had some huge-spending projects from 2010.

The interactivity of this exemplar is that you can zoom in and select specific departments, which shows you detailed data.
